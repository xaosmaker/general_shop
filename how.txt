this will be a general purpose app for coffee shop with delivery.
the user will login and can order a coffee or whatever the shop sell

the owner can own multiple shops and the shops will be intependent and the items 
they sell will be intependent.

this will have item agnostic categories and the owner can register any item
he want.

the items can have multiple variants with the choice of multiple select or select 
one variant.

the order placed will be accepted from the worker if the item has quantity the quantity
will reduced on the order accept after the payment.

there is and option for paying on delivery or paying with card.
